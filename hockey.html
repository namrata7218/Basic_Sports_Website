Hockey

Hockey is a fast-paced team sport played with a stick and a ball. It is the national sport of India and is played worldwide.

Basic Rules of Hockey

  • Each team has 11 players including a goalkeeper.
  • The match is divided into four quarters.
  • Goals can be scored only from inside the shooting circle.
  • Players must use only the flat side of the stick.
  • The team scoring more goals wins.
