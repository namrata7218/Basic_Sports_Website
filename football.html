Football

Football, also known as soccer, is the most popular sport in the world. It is played between two teams of eleven players using a round ball.

Basic Rules of Football

  • Each team has 11 players including a goalkeeper.
  • The match has two halves of 45 minutes each.
  • Only the goalkeeper can use hands inside the penalty area.
  • A goal is scored when the ball crosses the goal line.
  • The team with the most goals wins the match.
