Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It originated in England and is extremely popular in countries like India, Australia, and England.

Basic Rules of Cricket

  • Each team has 11 players.
  • One team bats while the other bowls and fields.
  • Runs are scored by hitting the ball and running between wickets.
  • A batsman can be out in several ways like bowled, caught, or run out.
  • The team with the highest runs wins the match.
